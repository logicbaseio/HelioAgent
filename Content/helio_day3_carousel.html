HELIO DEBT LAYER 03 // BROKEN SCHEMA
The Invisible Revenue Leak
NO SCHEMA =
NO RICH RESULTS =
FEWER CLICKS.
Schema markup drives star ratings, FAQ dropdowns, product prices, and event dates in search results. Without it, your listing looks bare next to competitors — even if you rank higher.
↓ Missing Product schema → no price/rating in SERP → lower click-through rate
↓ Missing FAQ schema → competitor's answer box appears instead of yours
↓ Missing Article schema → no date/author shown → users trust it less

HELIO DEBT LAYER 04 // INTERNAL LINKS
The PageRank Drain
BROKEN INTERNAL
LINKS DRAIN
YOUR AUTHORITY.
Internal links pass PageRank — authority — between your pages. Orphaned pages, broken links, and shallow link depth means your strongest pages aren't powering your weaker ones.
↓ Orphaned pages receive zero authority flow — invisible to Google regardless of quality
↓ Broken links waste crawl budget and create dead-ends that frustrate both users and bots
↓ Shallow depth means key pages are buried 4+ clicks from the homepage — undiscoverable
